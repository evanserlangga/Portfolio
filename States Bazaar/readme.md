# States Bazaar – Regional Market & Sales Performance Analysis

## Overview
States Bazaar is a retail company utilizing mock-up transactional data derived from a large-scale e-commerce platform to analyze regional market performance. This project focuses on understanding **seasonal demand patterns**, **regional performance disparities**, and **product-level profitability** across multiple regions to support more effective sales, marketing, and operational strategies.

## Objectives
- Identify seasonal sales and demand patterns
- Evaluate regional performance and growth potential
- Analyze product-level profitability and return behavior
- Provide actionable insights to support strategic decision-making

## Dataset
- Source: Mock-up transactional data based on a large-scale e-commerce platform
- Coverage:
  - Multi-region sales transactions
  - Product categories and sub-categories
  - Sales, profit, and return records
- Key metrics:
  - Recency
  - Frequency
  - Monetary value
  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Returns

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Data analysis and visualization tools
- Dashboarding and reporting techniques

## Methodology
1. **Data Preparation & Structuring**  
   - Cleaning and validation of transactional data  
   - Standardization of regional and temporal attributes

2. **Regional Market Analysis**  
   - Evaluation of key performance metrics across regions and time periods  
   - Comparison of sales volume, profitability, and return rates

3. **Seasonality Analysis**  
   - Identification of high- and low-demand periods  
   - Assessment of promotional and holiday-driven demand spikes

4. **Product-Level Performance Analysis**  
   - Analysis of demand, revenue, and profit across product categories  
   - Identification of high-volume and high-margin products

## Results
- **Seasonality**:  
  November–December identified as the peak season driven by promotions and holidays, while January–February consistently represents a low-demand period across all regions.

- **Regional Performance**:  
  - **West**: Strongest and most profitable region  
  - **East**: Rapid growth with high expansion potential  
  - **Central**: Stable sales volume but weak profitability, indicating cost inefficiencies  
  - **South**: Overall underperformance with early signs of recovery

- **Product Insights**:  
  - Phones identified as the flagship product category with high demand and revenue  
  - Copiers recognized as a high-margin niche product  
  - Clear gaps observed between demand, revenue, and profitability across categories
