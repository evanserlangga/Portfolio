# Bank Sampah Digital – Waste Collection & Customer Segmentation Analysis

## Overview
Bank Sampah Digital (BSD) is a technology-driven social enterprise established in 2020 that manages dry waste through a circular economy approach and community-based engagement. Operating across the Greater Serang area, BSD collaborates with more than 200 active waste bank units and serves over 4,000 participating households.

This project analyzes waste collection trends and fluctuations over time to identify strategic opportunities for business development and to enhance consumer participation and retention.

## Objectives
- Analyze waste collection patterns and temporal trends
- Segment consumers based on behavioral characteristics
- Identify key focus areas for improving customer engagement
- Support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualization

## Dataset
- Source: Internal operational data from Bank Sampah Digital
- Coverage:
  - Waste collection transactions
  - Consumer activity records
- Key variables include:
  - Transaction dates
  - Collection frequency
  - Waste value metrics

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Tableau
- Data cleaning & preparation tools

## Methodology
1. **Data Preparation**  
   - Data cleaning and validation  
   - Structuring datasets for analytical consistency and dashboard integration

2. **RFM Analysis**  
   - **Recency**: Time since last waste collection activity  
   - **Frequency**: Number of collection transactions  
   - **Monetary**: Total value generated from waste collection  
   - Customer segmentation based on RFM scoring to uncover behavioral patterns

3. **Visualization & Dashboard Development**  
   - Development of an interactive Tableau dashboard
   - Visualization of trends, segment distributions, and performance indicators

## Results
- Clear visualization of waste collection trends and seasonal fluctuations
- Identification of high-value and high-engagement customer segments
- Actionable insights to support strategic business development
- A dynamic dashboard that enables stakeholders to explore data interactively
